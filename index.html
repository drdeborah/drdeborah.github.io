Naturopathic Medicine

Naturopathic medicine is a distinct primary health care profession, emphasizing prevention, treatment, and optimal health through the use of therapeutic methods and substances that encourage your inherent self-healing process.

The practice of naturopathic medicine includes modern and traditional, scientific, and empirical methods. More »

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is one of the oldest forms of medicine. It is a gentle and effective treatment based upon the concept of energy flow (Qi) through meridians.

Fine needles are inserted on specific points along the meridians to balance the flow of Qi to resolve dysfunction, normalize physiology, and prevent or modify the perception of pain. More »

Traditional Chinese Medicine

TCM is a independent system of thought and medical practice used for over two thousand years to resolve dysfunction and promote health and well-being. TCM treats the pattern of your complete physiological and psychological condition.

We use TCM tools in your treatments, including acupuncture, herbs, moxibustion and cupping, to restore whole-body harmony and balance. More »

Hydrotherapy

Treatments are based upon the over two-hundred-year-old European healing traditions of using a variety of water-based therapies to stimulate the body’s own healing force.

We use constitutional hydrotherapy, warming sheet wraps and other hydrotherapeutic treatments. The temperature of the water, length of time, and the type of therapy used all have varying restorative effects on your body. More »

Telemedicine

Telemedicine consultations include custom treatment plans consisting of Naturopathic Medicine therapies and Chinese Herbal Medicine.

Includes lab test ordering, evaluation of lab results, consultations with current physicians regarding past medical history if needed, and treatment plan. More »

About

Dr Deborah Maken, PhD, ND, LAc

Brain Health Specialist

Dr Maken graduated from Bastyr University, Seattle with a Doctorate of Naturopathic Medicine. She has a PhD in Behavioral Neuroscience, and was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Columbia University's Department of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry in the Brain Imaging Laboratory in NYC.

Dr Maken is a licensed Naturopathic Doctor (ND) in California and Washington, and a licensed Acupuncturist (LAc) with a Master's of Traditional Oriental Medicine (MTOM) from Emperors College in Santa Monica, California. More »

Treatments

Dr Maken sees health participants with acute and chronic conditions and effectively treats with traditional medicine therapies and conventional scientific approaches. She also sees health participants for preventative care.

Common conditions treated in Dr Maken’s practice include:

  • Pain - Acute and Chronic
  • Bacterial, Viral, and Fungal Infections
  • Post-Viral/Bacterial Syndromes
  • Cardiovascular Issues
  • Immune Dysregulation
  • Osteoporosis/Osteopenia
  • Endocrine Conditions including Thyroid, Female/Male Hormonal, Diabetes, and Metabolic Syndrome
  • Gastrointestinal Issues
  • Fatigue/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome
  • Allergies
  • Skin Issues
  • Mental, Behavioral, and Emotional Conditions
  • Brain Health
