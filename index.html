About

Dr Deborah Maken, PhD, ND, LAc

Brain Health Specialist

Dr Maken graduated from Bastyr University, Seattle with a Doctorate of Naturopathic Medicine. She has a PhD in Behavioral Neuroscience, and was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Columbia University's Department of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry in the Brain Imaging laboratory in NYC.

Dr Maken is a licensed Naturopathic Doctor (ND) in California and Washington, and a licensed Acupuncturist (LAc) with a Master's of Traditional Oriental Medicine (MTOM) from Emperors College in Santa Monica, California.

Treatments

Dr Maken treats acute and chronic conditions with traditional medicines and conventional scientific approaches. She sees health participants for both preventative healthcare and acute conditions such as

  • bacterial and viral infections
  • post-viral syndrome
  • pain

as well as chronic conditions including

  • thyroid
  • diabetes
  • cardiovascular
  • immune
  • endocrine, hormonal, digestive
  • fatigue
  • allergies
  • skin
  • mental, behavioral, emotional
  • brain health
